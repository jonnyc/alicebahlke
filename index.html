Welcome

Biography

I was born and raised in central Florida with my older brother and sister. I earned my BA in Theater Studies at Emory University in Atlanta, GA and also trained at the O’Neill National Theater Institute in Waterford, CT, New York University in New York City, and the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Arts, UK.

I moved to New York City in 2009 where I first appeared onstage at a nightclub in midtown.  It was a two drink minimum and I was paid a salary of $5.  $5 did not cover one drink. Since then, I’ve appeared off-Broadway, in national commercials, and in independent films. I’ve also become a NYC DCA licensed Sightseeing Guide and a licensed bartender.   Thanks for visiting this site to check out more details.
